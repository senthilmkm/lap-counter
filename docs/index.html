Smart Features Built For Athletes

Orbit uses state-of-the-art smartphone sensors and algorithms to count laps automatically, hands-free.

🧭

Fused Gyro Heading Engine

Our specialized yaw projection calculations eliminate magnetic anomalies and compass warping caused by gym steel building frames. Tracks indoor loops flawlessly without beacons.

🚶

Step-Gate & Phase Tracking

Double-trigger protection gates lock in your count. Filters your gait dynamically, requiring 98% of expected lap steps and matching heading before register.

🗺️

Live Tracking & Maps

Acquire satellite locks instantly with pre-warming coordinates. Check your path live with Standard/Satellite toggles, and use map search to find coffee, restaurants, or stores nearby.

🔊

Hands-Free Voice Cues

Get real-time Text-to-Speech audio splits directly to your AirPods or headphones. Announces split lap times, cadence pacing, and progress goals automatically.

🔋

Pocket-Safe Background Task

Close the screen or place the phone in your pocket. Integrated background session tracking ensures the app is highly responsive, counting laps even while system tasks run in the background.

📊

Advanced Workout Analytics

Evaluate your fitness metrics in depth. Inspect live cadence rate, estimated stride length, cornering consistency, and export GPX or CSV files directly to Strava or Garmin.
