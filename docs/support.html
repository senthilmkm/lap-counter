Frequently Asked Questions

How does Orbit count indoor laps automatically?

Orbit incorporates smart gyroscope-only heading algorithms (fused yaw calculations) alongside your device's magnetometer and accelerometer. During the 5-second calibration phase, it saves a sensory "Point A" fingerprint. Whenever you return to that start line matching that heading and satisfying step verification gates, a lap is counted automatically.

What is the Step-Gate filter?

To prevent false triggers (like turning around mid-lap or walking back and forth near the start line), Orbit implements a step-gating filter. On subsequent laps, the detector stays locked until you complete at least 98% of your average estimated steps per lap.

My indoor track has steel columns that warp compass readings. Will it count laps?

Yes! Orbit features a "Fused Magnetic-Immune Heading" mode. It projects relative heading vectors exclusively using raw integrated gyroscope yaw. This makes Orbit completely immune to magnetic anomalies and metal distortion common in gym structures.

Does Outdoor GPS mode work in the background?

Yes. With background permissions granted, Orbit will continue tracking your coordinates, drawing your route polyline, and speaking lap progress even if your phone screen is locked or you slide the device into your pocket.

What is GPS Pre-Warming & Calibration Bypass?

Orbit pre-warms your GPS receiver immediately upon selecting Outdoor mode. If your phone achieves a high-accuracy satellite lock before you tap "Start", the app bypasses the default 45-second outdoor lock sequence entirely, allowing you to begin your workout instantly under 1 second.

How do I restore my Orbit Pro subscription on a new device?

Open the app, tap the Settings gear icon, scroll down, and select the subscription details. Tap the "Restore Purchases" button. If your App Store Apple Account holds an active subscription, Orbit Pro will unlock instantly.
