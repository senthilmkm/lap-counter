Phase 1 Done

Core Engine & GPS Optimization

Robust mathematical filters for indoor tracks and instant lock-times for outdoor routines.

  • Fused Magnetic-Immune Heading gyroscope-only integrated yaw projection eliminates compass warp in steel building frames.
  • BLE-Free Phase State Machine State cycle (armed ➔ away ➔ approaching ➔ counted) prevents early lap trigger at far turns.
  • 98% Step Gating & Heading Gate Filters triggers to return-to-start positions (gated until 98% expected steps and heading match).
  • Continuous Pre-Warming locks Saves location data immediately on mode select, preventing cold satellite resets on start.
  • Instant Calibration Bypass Direct coordinates transfer to reducer locks GPS under 1 second, removing the 45s wait.

Why the BLE-Free & Hot-Lock GPS Engines Win

Indoor trackers normally fail due to magnetometer attitude drift caused by steel structure anomalies. Our unique pure-gyroscope yaw projection isolates directional calculations from compass warp, yielding consistent accuracy. Outdoors, continuous pre-warming ensures that your session starts without a cold location-receiver delay.
